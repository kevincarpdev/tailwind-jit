About Us

Swimline Inc.

Swimline was founded in 1976 by Bob McCoy and is currently one of the oldest independent distributors in the country.

We are a full line distributor carrying all of the top brands including Pentair, Fluidra, Hayward and Raypak. We serve the gunite, vinyl and fiberglass industries and specialize in the service industry.

Why buy from us?  We pride ourselves in knowledgeable and helpful customer service.  Serving your needs as a dealer is what we do best.  We like to educate our customers and offer resources where needed to help you grow your business.

We also maintain a large parts inventory so that you are sure to get what you need without waiting for special orders.

Swimline has locations in Atlanta, Alpharetta and Kennesaw.  We can ship or deliver locally depending on your needs.
