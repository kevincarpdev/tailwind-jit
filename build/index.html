Description

With a 100% drain, clean-out port, and balanced hydraulic flow, the Clean & Clear Plus 420 cartridge filter offers simple, streamlined maintenance for your pool. Equipped with clamp rings for easy access to the filters, 2-inch plumbing for faster cleaning and maximum flow, and a 1-inch drain, making winterization a breeze.

The Clean & Clear Plus 420 cartridge system uses four nonwoven polyester cartridges that can be easily rinsed to reduce water consumption. This system is built with only the best, most durable materials and designed to maximize the filter’s surface area, significantly prolonging the filter’s life and extending the time between cleanings. Plus, get an increased capacity for dirt and debris collection from the pool water in the durable injection molded tank with a large filter area.

Clean & Clear Plus 420 cartridge filter offers an effective filtration area of 420 square feet and a flow rate of 150 GPM. Plus, it has a continuous high-flow internal air relief and is NSF listed with superior strength. Measures 22-inch length by 50-inch height by 22 ½-inch width—available only in-store from Pentair dealers or retail stores.
